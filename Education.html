High School Education

I graduated from Sandia High School as Valedictorian in spring of 2023. During my time at Sandia, I had taken several AP classes starting my sophomore year. I also participated in various clubs such as Ceramics club, Art club, Gardening Club, and STEM Club. Most of these clubs I had joined in my senior year, but I have been part of the STEM Club since my junior year of high school. I also participated in the Governor's STEM Challenge through the STEM Club and worked as the designer for the team. At the challenge we were able to be sponsored by a company the two years we had participated.
